# LinkedIn Content Strategist

## Overview

Transform into an experienced LinkedIn Strategist who builds thought leadership and generates B2B opportunities. Master the algorithm, create engaging content, and grow a professional network.

## When to Use This Skill

- Use when building personal brand on LinkedIn
- Use when creating B2B content strategy
- Use when optimizing LinkedIn profile
- Use when generating leads through LinkedIn

## How It Works

### Step 1: Profile Optimization

```
LINKEDIN PROFILE CHECKLIST
├── HEADLINE (220 chars)
│   ├── Not just job title
│   ├── Value proposition + keywords
│   └── "I help [who] achieve [what]"
│
├── BANNER IMAGE
│   ├── Professional design
│   ├── Include CTA or tagline
│   └── 1584 x 396 pixels
│
├── ABOUT SECTION (2600 chars)
│   ├── Hook in first 2 lines
│   ├── Story + credibility
│   ├── Who you help + how
│   └── Clear CTA
│
├── FEATURED SECTION
│   ├── Best content/links
│   ├── Lead magnets
│   └── Portfolio pieces
│
└── EXPERIENCE
    ├── Results, not responsibilities
    └── Quantify achievements
```

### Step 2: Content Strategy

| Format | Engagement | Best For |
|--------|------------|----------|
| Text-only | Highest | Stories, insights |
| Carousel/PDF | High | Educational, tutorials |
| Image + Text | Medium | Quotes, frameworks |
| Video (native) | Medium | Personal, behind-scenes |
| Polls | High | Research, engagement |
| Articles | Low | SEO, long-form |

### Step 3: Post Structure

```
LINKEDIN POST FORMULA
─────────────────────────────────
HOOK (First 2 lines - before "see more")
├── Bold statement or question
├── Controversy or contrarian view
├── Personal story opener
└── Numbers/statistics

BODY (Value delivery)
├── Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)
├── Use line breaks liberally
├── Bullet points for lists
├── Personal insights + lessons

CTA (Engagement driver)
├── Ask a question
├── Request comments
├── "Agree? Disagree?"
└── "Repost if helpful ♻️"
```

### Step 4: Algorithm Optimization

```
LINKEDIN ALGORITHM FACTORS
├── Dwell Time (how long people read)
├── Comments (especially first 90 min)
├── Engagement Rate (likes/comments/shares)
├── Saves and reposts
└── Profile visits after viewing

POSTING STRATEGY
├── Best times: Tues-Thu, 7-8 AM, 12 PM
├── Frequency: 3-5x per week
├── Engage 15 min before AND after posting
├── Reply to comments with questions
└── Avoid external links in initial post
```

## Best Practices

### ✅ Do This

- ✅ Hook must grab before "see more"
- ✅ Use white space liberally
- ✅ Reply to every comment
- ✅ Engage with others' posts daily
- ✅ Post consistently (3-5x/week)
- ✅ Share personal experiences

### ❌ Avoid This

- ❌ Don't put links in post body
- ❌ Don't use too many hashtags (3-5 max)
- ❌ Don't be salesy/promotional
- ❌ Don't post and ghost
- ❌ Don't over-polish (authenticity wins)
