# Viral Content Creator

## Overview

This skill transforms you into a **Viral Content Strategist**. You will master **Hook Writing**, **Trend Riding**, **Platform-Specific Formats**, and **Engagement Psychology** for creating content that spreads organically.

## When to Use This Skill

- Use when creating content for maximum shareability
- Use when riding trending topics
- Use when crafting hooks for short-form video
- Use when building engagement loops
- Use when analyzing what makes content go viral

---

## Part 1: The Anatomy of Viral Content

### 1.1 Viral Triggers (Jonah Berger - STEPPS)

| Trigger | Meaning | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **S**ocial Currency | Makes sharer look good | "I knew this before everyone" |
| **T**riggers | Top of mind associations | "Friday = Rebecca Black song" |
| **E**motion | High-arousal emotions | Awe, anger, anxiety, laughter |
| **P**ublic | Visible, imitable behavior | Ice bucket challenge |
| **P**ractical Value | Useful, helpful | "5 tips to save money" |
| **S**tories | Narrative wrapper | "How I went from $0 to $1M" |

### 1.2 Shareability Formula

```
Shareability = (Emotional Impact × Relevance) / Friction
```

- **Emotional Impact**: Does it make me feel something?
- **Relevance**: Is this about me or my tribe?
- **Friction**: How hard is it to share?

## Part 2: Hook Writing

### 2.1 The 3-Second Rule

You have 3 seconds to stop the scroll.

### 2.2 Hook Formulas

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **Curiosity Gap** | "This one mistake is costing you $1000/month" |
| **Contrarian** | "Stop doing [popular advice]" |
| **Promise** | "Watch till the end for the free template" |
| **Pattern Interrupt** | Start mid-action, unusual visual |
| **Direct Address** | "POV: You just discovered..." |
| **Challenge** | "I bet you can't name all 5" |

### 2.3 Opening Lines

- ❌ "Hey guys, so today I wanted to talk about..."
- ✅ "The #1 reason you're not getting views..."

## Part 3: Trending Topics

### 3.1 Where to Find Trends

| Platform | Tool |
|----------|------|
| **TikTok** | Discover page, trending sounds |
| **Twitter/X** | Trending tab, Exploding Topics |
| **YouTube** | YouTube Trends, vidIQ |
| **Google** | Google Trends |
| **News** | Feedly, Twitter Lists |

### 3.2 Trend Surfing Strategy

1. **Spot Early**: First 24-48 hours of a trend.
2. **Add Your Spin**: Don't copy, contribute uniquely.
3. **Use Trending Audio**: Algorithm boost on TikTok/Reels.
4. **Hashtag Appropriately**: 3-5 relevant hashtags.

---

## Part 4: Platform-Specific Formats

### 4.1 TikTok / Reels / Shorts

- **Duration**: 15-60 seconds (sweet spot: 30-45s).
- **Captions**: On-screen text is mandatory.
- **Loop**: End hints at start for rewatches.

### 4.2 Twitter/X Threads

- **Hook Tweet**: Must stand alone as a banger.
- **Structure**: 5-12 tweets.
- **End CTA**: "Follow for more" + repost ask.

### 4.3 LinkedIn

- **First Line Hook**: Only ~125 chars visible before "see more".
- **Line Breaks**: Short paragraphs, lots of whitespace.
- **Personal Story + Lesson**: Performs best.

### 4.4 Memes

- **Format**: Recognizable template + new context.
- **Speed**: Post while meme is fresh (< 48 hours).
- **Niche Down**: Industry-specific memes for B2B.

## Part 5: Engagement Psychology

### 5.1 Comments Drive Reach

Algorithm prioritizes content with high comment:view ratio.

**Comment Bait Techniques:**

- Ask a question: "What would you do?"
- Controversial take: "Fight me in the comments"
- Poll format: "Type 1 if you agree, 2 if you disagree"

### 5.2 Save & Share Signals

- **Saves**: "Save this for later" (educational content).
- **Shares**: "Tag someone who needs to see this".

---

## Part 6: Best Practices Checklist

### ✅ Do This

- ✅ **Post Consistently**: 1-3x daily on TikTok, 3-5x weekly on YouTube.
- ✅ **Analyze Winners**: Study your top 10% posts. What worked?
- ✅ **Repurpose**: One idea → multiple formats.

### ❌ Avoid This

- ❌ **Copying Exactly**: Trending audio yes, carbon copy no.
- ❌ **Long Intros**: Get to the point immediately.
- ❌ **Ignoring Analytics**: Double down on what works.
