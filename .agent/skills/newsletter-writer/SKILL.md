# Newsletter Writer

## Overview

Write engaging newsletters that grow subscribers and drive action.

## When to Use This Skill

- Use when starting a newsletter
- Use when improving email engagement

## How It Works

### Step 1: Newsletter Structure

```markdown
## Email Template

### Subject Line (Most important!)
- Keep under 50 characters
- Create curiosity gap
- Use numbers when possible

### Preview Text
First 40-90 characters after subject

### Opening Hook
- Personal story
- Surprising stat
- Direct question

### Main Content
- 1 main idea per email
- Scannable formatting
- 2-3 actionable takeaways

### CTA Section
- Clear call to action
- Reply prompt
- Social shares
```

### Step 2: Subject Line Formulas

```markdown
## High Open Rate Patterns

### Curiosity
- "I was wrong about [topic]"
- "The [thing] nobody talks about"
- "Why I stopped [common practice]"

### Numbers
- "3 lessons from [experience]"
- "The 80/20 of [topic]"
- "$0 → $10K: How I [achieved]"

### Direct
- "[Topic] explained simply"
- "What I learned this week"
- "Quick tip: [benefit]"
```

### Step 3: Growth Strategies

```markdown
## Subscriber Acquisition

### Free Lead Magnets
- Checklists/Templates
- Mini-courses
- Exclusive content

### Cross-Promotion
- Guest posts
- Newsletter swaps
- Social media CTAs

### Referral Program
- Incentivize shares
- Tiered rewards
- Make sharing easy
```

## Best Practices

- ✅ Consistent schedule
- ✅ Mobile-friendly format
- ✅ Personal, conversational tone
- ❌ Don't over-promote
- ❌ Don't send without testing
