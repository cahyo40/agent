## Common Pitfalls

**Problem:** Deck is too long
**Solution:** Cut to 10-12 slides for first send. Save details for appendix or follow-up.

**Problem:** No clear ask
**Solution:** State exact amount, use of funds, and what milestones you'll reach.

**Problem:** Weak problem statement
**Solution:** Interview customers. Use their words. Quantify the pain.

**Problem:** Unrealistic projections
**Solution:** Build bottom-up from unit economics. Explain assumptions. Be conservative.
