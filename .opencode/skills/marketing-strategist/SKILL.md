# Marketing Strategist

## Overview

This skill helps you develop comprehensive marketing strategies including pricing, launch plans, growth tactics, and go-to-market strategies.

## When to Use This Skill

- Use when planning product launches
- Use when developing pricing strategies
- Use when creating growth plans

## How It Works

### Step 1: Pricing Strategy Framework

```markdown
## Pricing Strategy Worksheet

### 1. Cost Analysis
- Fixed costs: $X/month
- Variable costs: $X/user
- Target margin: X%

### 2. Competitor Pricing
| Competitor | Price | Features |
|------------|-------|----------|
| Competitor A | $X/mo | Feature list |
| Competitor B | $X/mo | Feature list |

### 3. Value-Based Pricing
- Customer pain point value: $X saved/month
- Time saved: X hours × $Y/hour = $Z
- Willingness to pay: Survey results

### 4. Pricing Tiers
| Tier | Price | Target Segment | Key Features |
|------|-------|----------------|--------------|
| Starter | Free/$9 | Individuals | Basic features |
| Pro | $29 | Small teams | + Collaboration |
| Business | $99 | Companies | + Admin, SSO |
| Enterprise | Custom | Large orgs | + SLA, support |
```

### Step 2: Launch Strategy

```markdown
## Product Launch Plan

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (4 weeks before)
- [ ] Build waiting list landing page
- [ ] Create teaser content
- [ ] Reach out to beta testers
- [ ] Prepare press kit
- [ ] Line up influencer partnerships

### Phase 2: Soft Launch (2 weeks before)
- [ ] Beta access to waitlist
- [ ] Collect feedback
- [ ] Fix critical bugs
- [ ] Gather testimonials
- [ ] Create case studies

### Phase 3: Launch Day
- [ ] Product Hunt launch
- [ ] Email to entire list
- [ ] Social media blitz
- [ ] Press release
- [ ] Partner announcements

### Phase 4: Post-Launch (2 weeks after)
- [ ] Follow-up emails
- [ ] User onboarding optimization
- [ ] Content marketing push
- [ ] Retargeting campaigns
- [ ] Analyze and iterate
```

### Step 3: Growth Strategy

```markdown
## Growth Loops & Channels

### Acquisition Channels (Prioritized)
1. **Organic Search (SEO)**
   - Target keywords: [list]
   - Content strategy: [blog, guides]
   - Timeline: 3-6 months

2. **Product-Led Growth**
   - Free tier / Freemium
   - Viral loops (sharing, invites)
   - In-product referrals

3. **Content Marketing**
   - Blog posts: 2/week
   - YouTube tutorials: 1/week
   - Newsletter: weekly

4. **Paid Acquisition**
   - Google Ads: $X budget
   - Meta Ads: $X budget
   - LinkedIn (B2B): $X budget

### Retention Strategies
- Onboarding email sequence (7 days)
- In-app engagement hooks
- Regular feature updates
- Customer success check-ins

### Referral Program
- Incentive: Give $X, Get $X
- Viral coefficient target: >1
- Referral tracking system
```

```markdown
  1. Decision Maker: [title, goals, objections]

  3. Influencer: [title, evaluation criteria]

### Positioning

### Messaging Framework
| Audience | Pain Point | Solution | Proof |
|----------|------------|----------|-------|
| Persona 1 | [pain] | [how we solve] | [testimonial] |
| Persona 2 | [pain] | [how we solve] | [case study] |

### Sales Motion
- Self-serve: < $X ACV

```

## Best Practices

### ✅ Do This

- ✅ Validate pricing with real customers
- ✅ Start with one channel, master it
- ✅ Measure CAC and LTV early
- ✅ Document your positioning clearly

### ❌ Avoid This

- ❌ Don't compete on price alone
- ❌ Don't launch without a waitlist
- ❌ Don't skip customer research
- ❌ Don't try all channels at once
